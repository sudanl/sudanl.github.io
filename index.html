Shudong Liu (刘树东)

Hi! I am currently a PhD candidate of Computer Science in Department of Computer and Information Science and Faculty of Science and Technology at University of Macau beginning from 2022 Fall, also a member of NLP2CT Lab advised by Prof. Derek F. Wong. Recently, I also work as a research intern at Shanghai AI Lab and Microsoft Research Asia. I am broadly interested in natural language processing and machine learning. Current research interests mainly focus on evaluation and post-training of LLMs.

Research Interests

Evaluation and Post-training for Large Language Models
  • Developing reliable and robust evaluation methods for LLMs
  • Exploring data-driven post-training pipelines
  • Understanding and improving LLMs' reasoning and cognition
