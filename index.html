Shudong Liu | 刘树东

I am currently a first-year PhD student of Computer Science in the Faculty of Science and Technology at the University of Macau, also a member of NLP2CT Lab advised by Prof. Derek F. Wong. I have a broad interest in various fields of natural language processing (NLP), especially machine translation (currently) and dialogue systems (before). If you would like to have a discussion or make friends with me, please feel free to contact me by email (sudanl[AT]foxmail.com).
